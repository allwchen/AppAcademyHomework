Description

Wikipedia article

This is a spicy tofu dish common in Chinese restaurants

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Chili Garlic Sauce
  • Black Bean Sauce
  • Minced Pork
  • Green Onion
  • Ginger

Directions

  1. Cut up tofu into cubes
  2. Stir fry pork
  3. Add sauces and ginger
  4. Add tofu
  5. Sprinkle with green onion
